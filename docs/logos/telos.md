# Telos: The Idealized Form of the Personal Knowledge Database

**Status:** PROPOSED
**Dialect:** FORMAL_LOGIC

## 1. The Vision
The Personal Knowledge Database (PKB) is not a mere archive; it is a **living organ** of the extended mind. Its purpose is the **apotheosis of memory**—transforming transient conversations into a stable, structured, and semantically dense substrate for future intelligence.

## 2. Theoretical Grounding
- **Atomization:** Knowledge must be broken into its smallest irreducible units to allow for novel recombinations.
- **Semantic Density:** Every unit must be enriched with metadata, embeddings, and cross-references to ensure it is never "lost" in the noise.
- **Autopoiesis:** The system should eventually participate in its own maintenance and evolution (Phase 6+).

## 3. The ALL-SEEING-AUDITOR (Vigiles Aeternae)
The Auditor is the **immune system** of the PKB. It ensures that the transition from raw input (The World) to refined knowledge (The Organ) maintains absolute structural integrity.

- **Vigiles Aeternae:** The eternal watch over the database state.
- **Theatrum Mundi:** The representation of all captured knowledge as a coherent "theater" of reality.

## 4. Success Metrics
- **Zero Loss:** Every significant insight from a conversation is captured.
- **High Recall:** Semantic search retrieves exactly what is needed, even with vague queries.
- **Structural Purity:** No orphaned units, no broken links, no redundant atoms.
