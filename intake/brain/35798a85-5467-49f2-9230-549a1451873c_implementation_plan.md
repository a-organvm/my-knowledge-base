## Beyond No-Contact: Product Expansions

The relationship recovery space is massive. By utilizing our existing architecture (staking, hardware oracles, peer-review), we can build out these facets immediately:

1. **The "Endorphin Recovery" Stake (Re-using our Fitness Tech)**
   *   *Core Problem*: Depression and lethargy post-breakup.
   *   *Mechanic*: We keep the HealthKit/Google Fit bridges! Users stake money that they will walk 5,000 steps a day or hit the gym 3x a week to physically process the cortisol/grief. If they stay in bed all week, they lose their stake.
2. **The "Digital Detox" Stake (Screen Time Limit)**
   *   *Core Problem*: Doom-scrolling the ex's Instagram or TikTok.
   *   *Mechanic*: Utilizing the iOS `DeviceActivity` / Screen Time APIs, users stake money that they will keep their Instagram usage under 15 minutes a day.
3. **The "Venting Vault" (Diversion Therapy)**
   *   *Core Problem*: The overwhelming urge to text the ex late at night.
   *   *Mechanic*: A feature where they type the toxic message they *want* to send, but they send it to the Styx app instead. The app logs it as a "Successful Diversion," keeping their stake safe and releasing the urge. 
4. **"The Sponsor" (Peer Support)**
   *   *Core Problem*: Isolation during the withdrawal phase of a breakup.
   *   *Mechanic*: Utilizing the Tavern/Fury system, but pairing users up. If one user feels weak, they hit the "SOS" button, and their matched peer has 10 minutes to talk them down, earning part of a community reward pool.

## AI Research Synthesis: Strategic Imperatives

Based on the execution of the four AI research workstreams, we must integrate the following core strategic pillars into the Styx architecture to ensure product-market fit and absolute efficacy:

### 1. Market Positioning: Luxurification and the "Blockchain of Truth"
*   **The Problem:** Current mental health apps suffer a 96% abandonment rate by Day 15 because they rely on intrinsic motivation and generic "therapy-speak," failing to survive the neurobiological "extinction burst."
*   **The Pivot:** Styx must be positioned as an elite, high-friction **Commitment Device** (the "75 Hard" for the mind). We must utilize zero-UI, luxury design paradigms, completely abandoning therapy-speak ("healing") in favor of executive performance lexicon ("mastering emotional regulation," "digital sovereignty").
*   **Anti-Tamper Infrastructure:** To be un-cheatable, Styx must go beyond basic Screen Time APIs. We must implement **Continuous Runtime Attestation** (e.g., Approov/Guardsquare) to block rooted devices, OCR screen analysis to prevent browser-bypasses, and multi-layered geolocation (GPS + Wi-Fi Triangulation) to detect spoofing.

### 2. Loss Aversion Mechanics: Dynamic Staking
Psychological pain points during a breakup are highly predictable. Static stakes are insufficient. We must implement:
*   **The Weekend Multiplier:** Stakes automatically double (2x) from Friday 5:00 PM to Sunday 9:00 AM to combat the loss of cognitive load and the "Default Mode Network" rumination that causes weekend relapses.
*   **Lockdown Mode (Days 3 & 21):** During the acute panic of Day 3 and the dopamine trough of Day 21, the smart contract becomes fully immutable. The user cannot lower their stakes or pause the contract.
*   **The Rationalization Cooldown (24-Hour Time-Lock):** If a user claims a "logistical emergency" requires them to break No Contact, the app enforces a 24-hour mandatory delay before releasing the communication block, forcing them to outlast the emotional wave.

### 3. Digital Exhaust: Zero-Trust Verification
Because direct SMS/Call log access is prohibited by OS sandboxing, we must rely entirely on "Digital Exhaust" to verify No Contact:
*   **Geofenced Proximity Stakes:** Micro-penalties applied for breaching a GPS radius around the ex-partner's locations.
*   **Kinematic Agitation Thresholds:** Using the accelerometer to detect the severe pacing/agitation that frequently precedes a text relapse, triggering an immediate app lockdown or "SOS" peer intervention.
*   **Shared Environment Deterrents:** Scanning Wi-Fi BSSIDs/SSIDs to detect if the user is occupying the same specific micro-environment (e.g., a specific coffee shop or apartment building) as the ex-partner.

### 4. Enterprise Expansion: B2B2C
*   **The Model:** While B2C scaling is hindered by App Store compliance (Guideline 5.1.2i), the B2B Executive Coaching market is ideal. Executive coaches ($2k-$10k/month engagements) fund the Styx smart contract as part of their fee. If the executive fails the behavioral protocol, the escrow is forfeited.
*   **Regulatory Moat:** To avoid "unlicensed gambling" classifications, forfeited funds must never return to Styx as profit. They must be routed to a DAO treasury, used to burn utility tokens, or donated to politically neutral 501(c)(3) charities.
*   **Behavioral Surplus:** The anonymized metadata (relapse velocity, financial staking vs. success rates, kinematic breakdown patterns) represents a highly lucrative secondary market for clinical research and algorithmic dating platforms.
