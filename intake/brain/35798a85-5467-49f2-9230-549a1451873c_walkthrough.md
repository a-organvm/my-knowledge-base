# Styx Phase 1 Pivot: Relationship Recovery & "No Contact"

This document outlines the changes made to the Styx architecture and codebase to successfully execute the Phase 1 B2C pivot from a general habit/fitness tracking application to a **"No Contact" relationship recovery platform**.

## Executive Summary

Based on strategic alignment and market intent, Styx is optimizing for an initial test pool of 18,000 highly motivated users in the relationship recovery space. We have refactored the core mechanism away from Apple HealthKit/Google Fit integrations and toward "Digital Exhaust" and "Weaponized Whistleblowers."

## Completed Workstreams

### 1. Verification Engine: The Weaponized Whistleblower
The "Blockchain of Truth" cannot rely on users to self-report "No Contact."
- **[NEW] Database Schema**: Added `002_whistleblower_bounty.sql` migration to introduce the `bounties` table and add a `bounty_link_id` to the `contracts` table. 
- **[MODIFIED] Contract Creation**: When a user creates a `RECOVERY_NOCONTACT` contract via `ContractsService`, the system now automatically generates a unique, anonymous cryptographic Bounty Link.
- **[NEW] Bounty Claim API**: Introduced a new, unauthenticated endpoint `POST /contracts/bounty/:linkId` in the `ContractsController`.
- **[NEW] Dispute Resolution Flow**: An Ex-partner (or third party) who receives the Bounty link can submit a screenshot claiming breach of contract. This proof is injected directly into the Fury Network for peer review, bypassing the user's ability to delete evidence.

### 2. Aegis Protocol: Psychological Guardrails
We have stripped out physical medical guardrails (BMI/Velocity) designed for fitness, replacing them with psychological limits to prevent emotional self-harm.
- **[REMOVED]**: `AegisProtocolService.validateHealthMetrics()` (which checked BMI thresholds).
- **[NEW]**: `AegisProtocolService.validatePsychologicalGuardrails()`.
- **Logic Inserted**:
  - **Max Stake Limit ($500)**: Prevents impulsive "revenge staking" during high-emotion phases (Day 1-3 of a breakup).
  - **Min Duration (7 Days)**: Enforces minimum time required to interrupt habituated neural pathways.
  - **Downward Spiral Prevention**: If a user fails 3 times consecutively, their maximum allowed stake is strictly capped at $50.
  - **Integrity Multipliers**: Users with low peer-trust scores are restricted from underwriting large stakes.

### 3. Frontend & Terminology Reskin
The UI has been modified to reflect the urgency and executive tone of the new target audience.
- **[MODIFIED] Landing Page (`src/web/app/page.tsx`)**: The hero manifesto was rewritten to focus on the "No Contact rule," "emotional resilience," and the "Weaponized Whistleblower / Ex Bounty" replacing the fitness features.
- **[MODIFIED] Onboarding (`src/web/components/OnboardingWizard.tsx`)**: Replaced the generic `OATH_CATEGORIES` with targeted relationship recovery streams: *No Contact (Absolute Severance)*, *Digital Detox*, *Endorphin Recovery*, and *Venting Vault*.

### 4. Codebase Trimming
To accelerate the MVP release schedule, unneeded legacy code was removed.
- **[DELETED]**: `src/mobile/services/HealthKitService.ts`
- **[DELETED]**: `src/mobile/services/GoogleFitService.ts`
- *Note:* These files are preserved in the git history and can be resurrected for Phase 2 "Endorphin Recovery" features.

### 5. Architectural Alignment
Documentation has been updated to provide a single source of truth for all future development and AI agents working on the repository.
